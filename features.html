Core Features

Four Pillars of CC Aware

Built for the speed and complexity of modern public safety — each pillar addresses a critical gap in legacy command center operations.

01

Single Pane of Glass

A unified browser-based geospatial map (built on ESRI ArcGIS) aggregates 911 CAD data, GPS unit positions from APX radios, live weather radar (including fire weather layers), LPR hits, and IoT sensor feeds simultaneously. Analysts see everything — no tab switching, no screen juggling.

ESRI ArcGISPremierOne CADAVL / GPSWeather OverlayLPR
02

Enhanced View — Live Video

Merges feeds from Avigilon fixed cameras, body-worn cameras (M500, V700, SVX), in-car video, and drone streams directly onto the map. Operators click any camera icon to pull the live feed. Drone-as-First-Responder programs stream aerial video before ground units arrive.

AvigilonM500 / V700Drone DFRRTSP StreamsBody Cam
03

AI Assist & Rules Engine

Avigilon AI analytics detect events — gunshots, crowds, vehicles of interest — and instantly trigger Rules Engine responses: surfacing the nearest camera feeds, alerting supervisors, and launching Missions workflows. Drastically reduces analyst cognitive load during high-stress incidents.

Avigilon AnalyticsRules EngineAuto Camera PullRave Panic ButtonShotSpotter
04

Cloud-Based & Secure

FedRAMP Authorized (Moderate) and CJIS-compliant cloud architecture. Accessible from any device with a browser — including mobile. Meets the federal security standards required for criminal justice data. No client software to install or maintain, enabling fast deployment.

Competitive Edge

What Sets CC Aware Apart

Two proprietary capabilities no competitor offers natively — built from Motorola's closed-loop hardware-software ecosystem.

Rules Engine

Automated Intelligence at Incident Speed

  • →Instant Rule Triggering — When a Rave Panic Button is pressed, a ShotSpotter alert fires, or a CAD incident is created, a pre-configured rule activates immediately — no dispatcher action required.
  • →Auto Camera Pull — The nearest live camera feeds are instantly surfaced on the operator's screen, correlated by GPS distance to the incident location.
  • →Cognitive Relief — Analysts monitoring 20+ feeds are guided by automated prioritization, reducing decision fatigue and response errors during high-pressure incidents.
  • →Customizable per Agency — Each agency configures its own rule library based on local incident types, geography, and operational protocols.
Missions Feature

Orchestrated Multi-Agency Response

  • →Standardized Workflows — Pre-built mission templates guide analysts through the right steps for each incident type — wildfire, active shooter, pursuit — in real time.
  • →Resource Tracking — Field unit GPS positions, assignments, and status updates tracked live within the mission view — no radio check-ins required.
  • →AI Rules Embedded — AI Assist functions are wired directly into active mission workflows, not bolted on separately.
  • →ROI-Proof Summary Logs — Every mission auto-generates a comprehensive after-action log, giving agencies auditable data for budget reviews and grant applications.
🛰️
New Integration

ICEYE SAR Satellite Imagery

Motorola Solutions partnered with ICEYE to integrate Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) satellite imagery directly into CC Aware. SAR sees through smoke, darkness, and cloud cover — giving incident commanders real-time fire perimeter data even when aerial assets are grounded. Critical for California wildfire command.
