What Is CommandCentral Aware?

The Brain of the Command Center

CommandCentral Aware is Motorola Solutions' cloud-based situational awareness and operations management platform. It aggregates data from CAD systems, radio networks, video cameras, IoT sensors, weather APIs, and AI analytics into a single browser-based geospatial map.

Designed for 911 dispatch centers, Real-Time Crime Centers (RTCCs), and multi-agency command posts, CC Aware replaces fragmented multi-screen workflows. It is part of the broader CommandCentral Suite — Motorola's end-to-end command center software platform.

Because Motorola already supplies radios, cameras, and CAD systems to most US public safety agencies, CC Aware layers on as a cloud software upgrade — not a rip-and-replace.
