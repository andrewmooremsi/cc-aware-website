Campaign Structure

Four Phases, One Pipeline

Each phase moves the target buyer — fire chief, IT director, or county emergency manager — one step closer to a qualified demo request with the Motorola field sales team.

01

Awareness — Digital Presence

Weeks 1–2 · LinkedIn & Google Ads

  • LinkedIn Sponsored Content targeting CA public safety titles (Fire Chief, Deputy Chief, Emergency Manager, IT Director)
  • Google Search Ads targeting "wildfire situational awareness software," "public safety command center," and "CAD video integration"
  • Retargeting pixel placed on motorolasolutions.com CC Aware product pages
  • Campaign landing page with wildfire scenario video and demo request form
Phase GoalDrive 500+ impressions among CA fire department decision-makers; 40+ landing page visits
02

Education — Email Nurture

Weeks 2–4 · Email Sequence

  • 3-email nurture sequence to existing Motorola customer contacts in CA fire segment
  • Email 1: Wildfire challenge framing — "What the Ventura County fires revealed about command center blind spots"
  • Email 2: Product walkthrough — three mission phases with CC Aware feature mapping
  • Email 3: Competitor comparison + FedRAMP compliance brief + demo offer
  • 3.5% click-through rate target; high-SQL quality scoring on demo form submissions
Phase Goal3.5% CTR on email sequence; move MQLs to demo-ready SQL stage
03

Engagement — Experience Center

Weeks 3–6 · Live Demo Events

  • Motorola Experience Center events in Los Angeles and Sacramento — full CC Aware wildfire simulation
  • Partner activation with California Fire Chiefs Association (CFCA) conference presence
  • Hands-on scenario: Pre-fire positioning → active wildfire response → post-incident reporting
  • Side-by-side demo vs. legacy multi-screen workflow to demonstrate cognitive load reduction
  • On-site ROI calculator session with Motorola account executives
Phase Goal15 qualified demo attendees from target CA fire department accounts
04

Conversion — Sales Close

Weeks 5–8 · Proposal & Close

  • Customized ROI proposal for each demo attendee, showing CC Aware cost vs. current multi-vendor spend
  • Compliance package delivery: FedRAMP authorization letter, CJIS security addendum, and procurement fast-track documentation
  • Internal champion enablement: slide deck + one-pager for fire chiefs to present to county IT and budget committees
  • Contract modification pathway briefing — showing how CC Aware can be added to existing Motorola agreements
  • 15% demo-to-SQL conversion target; pipeline goal of 3 qualified opportunities
Phase Goal15% demo-to-close conversion; 3 qualified pipeline opportunities opened
